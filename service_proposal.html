Service Proposal

*Services do not include conditioner and/or stain preventor/inhibitor, which may be added up to twice per year. Services do not include salt. Salt is an additional charge.

*Service excludes repairs, tile cleaning, filter tear downs, vandalism clean up or storm clean up. It also does not include rebalancing of chemicals if the pool has been drained and refilled for any reason. Service may be canceled at anytime with a 30 day written notice. There cannot be any restrictions as to when the pool/spa can be serviced. Service price reflects two weeks off per year. Minor parts and/or labor not to exceed $40.00 may be done as needed without prior authorization to keep the pool in proper running order. There will be no credit to your account for "Lock-outs" or missed visits beyond our control. Filters must be run a minimum 8 hrs.(Summer) and 4 hrs.(Winter) per day to ensure proper maintenance. Water level control is solely the customers responsibility. Crystal Clear Pool Service is not liable for plaster or tile stains, calcium build up or discoloration, we have no control over the chemical balance of fresh water coming into the pool, nor can we prevent them due to the aging of the tile and plaster.(All services charges are billed in advance)
